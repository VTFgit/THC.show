What are the prerequisites to join VMware Threat Hunting Challenge (THC) event?

  • "The more you do, the more you can achieve."
  • There are no requirements to join THC event. However, knowing fundamentals of VMware security solutions such as Carbon Black, NSX will increase your chances to earn more points.
  • But all you need is a positive mindset and a lot of patience to hunt for threats.

How can I get prepared for VMware Threat Hunting Challenge (THC) event?

    There are many ways VMware offers and enables you for the THC event.
  • 1. VMware TestDrive - VMware TestDrive, you can get hands-on user experience and get familiar with VMware security solutions prior to the THC event. Try the latest threat huntingTestDrive - TestDrive
  • 2. VMware HOL - VMware Lab Platform,Carbon Black Cloud Workload Protection or Carbon Black Endpoint Standard
  • 3. VMware TechZone - VMware | Carbon Black Tech Zone
  • 4. VMware YouTube channels - VMware Carbon Black
